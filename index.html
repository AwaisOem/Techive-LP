Our Aim

Techive: Innovate and Learn

Our aim at Techive is to create a vibrant community where app developers, game developers, web developers, AI enthusiasts, and marketers can come together to shape the future of technology. We strive to foster an environment of continuous learning, collaboration, and innovation.

Our Goals

Maximize Your Potential with Techive

At Techive, our goal is to create a vibrant community where tech enthusiasts can connect, collaborate, and grow. By providing access to resources, mentorship, and a network of like-minded individuals

Our Mission

Empowering Innovators

Techive aims to provide a dynamic platform for developers and tech enthusiasts to collaborate, learn, and innovate. By offering a diverse range of groups in app development, game development, web development, AI, and marketing.

Workshops

Organize intensive training sessions on specific topics like AI, blockchain technology, cybersecurity, and data science.

Competitions

Host hackathons, coding competitions, and game development contests to encourage innovation .

Tech News and Updates

Keep members informed about the latest trends, tools, and technologies through newsletters, blog posts, and social media updates.

Mentorship Programs

Create mentorship programs where experienced members can guide and support newcomers or those looking to advance their skill

Collaboration Projects

Facilitate collaborative projects among members, encouraging them to work together on real-world problems and build a portfolio.

Internships

Partner with companies for internships, sponsored projects, and collaborative research and much more.

Techive is a student-led society at the University of Sargodha that focuses on various technology domains, including app development, game development, web development, AI, marketing, and more.

Any student at the University of Sargodha with an interest in technology and a desire to learn and collaborate is welcome to join Techive.

You can join Techive by filling out the form located at the bottom of our website and submitting it.

You can contact us through our website, social media channels, or by reaching out to our committee members directly. We are always here to help!
About Us

Why Techive Stands Out

Welcome to Techive, the leading tech society at the University of Sargodha. We unite enthusiasts in app development, game development, web development, AI, and marketing. Join us to learn, share, and grow together in the world of technology.
